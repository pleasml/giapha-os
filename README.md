giapha-os is an open-source app for managing your family tree. It gives you an easy way to see family relationships, manage family members, and search or filter through the list.

---

## 📦 What is giapha-os?

giapha-os helps you build and view your family tree on your Windows computer. You can:

- See visual diagrams of family connections.
- Add, edit, or remove family members.
- Search by name or other details.
- Filter members by their generation or relation.

This app works offline after download. It stores your data on your computer to keep your information private.

## 👪 Using giapha-os

Here is how to use the main features:

### View Family Tree

- The main screen shows a visual tree diagram.

- Each member is a box connected to others showing relationships.

### Add Members

- Click **Add Member**.

- Enter details like name, birthdate, and relation.

- Save the member to add them to the tree.

### Edit or Remove Members

- Right-click a member's box or select a member from the list.

- Choose **Edit** to change their information.

- Choose **Remove** to delete the member.

### Search and Filter

- Use the search bar at the top to enter a name.

- Use filters to show members by generation or branch.
